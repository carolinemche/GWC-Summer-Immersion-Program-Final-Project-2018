Our Mission
The idea behind GetTech'd is that young girls who are interested in the technology sector can have access to resources to be more informed. We want girls to network with women in tech, ask advice, and have the opportunity to be involved. Our main goal is closing the gender gap and letting girls in tech know they aren’t alone in this male dominated field
